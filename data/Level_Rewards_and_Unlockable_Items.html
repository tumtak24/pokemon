Every level your trainer gains in Pokemon Go will result in Level Rewards and Unlockable Items that change based on the trainer's current level. This page is used to track which items you receive, and the level they unlock.

Note that certain levels also unlock specific Items that will then become obtainable at PokeStops, including Razz Berries, higher tiers of Poke Balls, and Potions.
