This page contains information on how to get and use Lucky Eggs in Pokemon Go.

Lucky Eggs are consumable items that let the player's trainer earn double the amount of experience for all actions performed for a total of 30 minutes.

How to Get Lucky Eggs

Lucky Eggs can be purchased in the Shop in exchange for PokeCoins.

1 Lucky Egg
 80 PokeCoins
8 Lucky Eggs
 500 PokeCoins
25 Lucky Eggs
 1,250 PokeCoins

Certain experience levels gained will also Reward you with Lucky Eggs, like achieving Level 9.

It's important to note that this time can count down whether or not you are online, so make sure to pick the best time to use it so that you can capitalize on your experience gain.

How to Use Lucky Eggs

When activated, this item will double all XP earned for 30 minutes! Combine this with Incense or a Lure Module and you'll be raking in XP just by catching lots of Pokemon. Make sure you've stocked up on Poke Balls before doing this, either at PokeStops or the Shop! 

Plan to evolve lots of Pokemon and explore new areas saturated with PokeStops before activating a Lucky Egg to ensure you'll make the most of it!

This is also a great time to have one or two egg incubuators close to hatching, as you'll also get a burst of XP.

To really reap in the benefits, you could capture a lot of common Pokemon, like Pidgey or Zubat, that require relatively few Candy to evolve. Evolving a Pokemon already nets you the most XP, so evolving Pokemon with a Lucky Egg grants you 1,000 XP a pop! New pokemon will also give a bonus, so combining lots of common pokemon to evolve with a couple high level new pokemon you've been waiting to evolve can make a great combo.

The Step-by-Step Process:

  • Step 1: Hoard Pokemon

Collect easy-to-catch-and-evolve Pokemon, like Pidgey. Pidgey are easy to come by and only cost 12 Pidgey Candy to evolve. The more the better!

  • Step 2: Plan Accordingly

If you have Pokemon that you know will evolve into something you haven't caught before, save them for your Lucky Egg XP spree! Also, travel to an area with lots of PokeStops, like a popular park, if you think you'll have time left after evolving Pokemon. It'll take about 5 minutes to evolve 10 Pokemon.

  • Step 3: Activate the Lucky Egg

You will now get double XP for every thing you do for 30 minutes.

  • Step 4: Get to Evolving!
  • Step 5: Use your remaining time to catch Pokemon
    This is why we traveled to an area with lots of PokeStops! If you have any time left over after your evolving-spree, attach a Lure Module to a PokeStop, activate an Incense, and get to catching. If you have the resources and are in a spot with multiple PokeStops next to each other, use a few Lure Modules to maximize the amount of Pokemon that appear! You can do this same thing pre-Lucky Egg as a way to stock up on Pokemon and Poke Balls, too. 

With a Lucky Egg activated, each evolved Pokemon will grant you 1,000 XP! If you're evolving a Pokemon into one you haven't caught before, you'll get 2,000 XP! Catching a Pokemon will only net you 200 XP, but if you catch a new Pokemon, you'll get 1,200 XP.
