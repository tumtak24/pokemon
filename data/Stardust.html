Stardust is an item that can be used to power up your Pokemon, increasing its CP and HP. It cannot be purchased in the shop, and so must be earned by performing certain actions within the game.

There are a few methods you can use to earn Stardust:

Catching Pokemon

Each time you catch a Pokemon, you'll be awarded 100 Stardust and three Pokemon Candy, specific to the Pokemon you just caught. 

Defending a Gym

If you have placed a Pokemon to defend your team's Gym, you can collect a Defender Bonus of Stardust and PokeCoins every 20 hours. See the Teams and Gyms page to learn more about this mechanic. 

Stardust is an incredibly important resource. Use it wisely and obtain as much of it as you can!
