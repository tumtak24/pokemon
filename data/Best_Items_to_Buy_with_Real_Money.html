Think your needs are a little more niche? Check out these suggestions on what to purchase if... 

If you want to make friends

It's been said that Lure Modules are better at attracting people than Pokemon, for good reason...


Lure Modules work for all trainers in the vicinity of a PokeStop for 30 minutes, and if someone is a few blocks away, they can tell if one is activated by the cherry blossom petals that surround the PokeStop on the map. Free extra Pokemon encounters for all! Who wouldn't be attracted to that? 

With the $9.99 1,200 PokeCoin bundle, you can get 13 Lure Modules and still have some PokeCoins left over. That's six and a half hours of people and Pokemon luring goodness! Take advantage of the crowds and connect with fellow trainers, preferably at a usually well-populated park during the day. Pick a place with a lot of Pokemon points-of-interest nearby, like Gyms, to maximize the traffic your Lure Module brings in.


If you're a hoarder

So, you're surrounded by PokeStops, and hit them multiple times a day? Can't bear to part with your Potions even though you're approaching Level 20, just in case?

What? You have how many Rattata?

It's okay, really. You should always be prepared for trouble, and every Pokemon is special and unique in its own way! You're just going to need a bigger bag to hold everything in.

A Bag Upgrade and Pokemon Storage Upgrade increase your item or Pokemon storage capacity by 50 and cost 200 PokeCoins each. Luckily, each 50 slot upgrade costs the same, so you can really increase the size of your storage with the $9.99 bundle of 1,200 PokeCoins.

If you care about your little Pokemon friends and want to save them from the soul-crushing fate of being ground up into Pokemon Candy, you could invest entirely in Pokemon Storage Upgrades and increase your capacity from 250 to 550! Save all the Pokemon!

More interested in the utility of having every item at your fingertips whenever you need it? Invest entirely in Bag Upgrades, and increase your capacity from a measly 350 to a ridiculous 650.

You may possibly need a stronger build to carry that bag now. How are you not over-encumbered?

If You Have Little Time To Play

Time is PokeCoins, and you don't have a lot of it. Between work, school, chores, and that bug infestation that really needs your attention, you can't spend hours a day exploring with Pokemon GO. So what do you do with the time you do have?

Be as efficient as possible with items! If you can set aside a single hour, you can make a lot of progress. This also applies to those who can't get to a high-activity Pokemon GO areas often. Here's how with $9.99 worth of PokeCoins:

With 1,200 PokeCoins in hand, purchase an eight-pack of Incense and an eight-pack of Lucky Eggs, both 500 PokeCoins. That's four hours of double-XP and extra Pokemon Encounters!

With the leftover 200 PokeCoins, you can buy another pair of a Lucky Egg and an Incense, a Bag Upgrade if you never have enough space for Items, or an Egg Incubator if you always have too many Eggs and rarely have time to walk to hatch them.

Tip: If you have more than one Egg Incubator on hand, always use your infinite-use Incubator on the 2km Eggs. This way, you'll get the most use out of it!

When you do have time to play, try to head to an area with lots of PokeStops around. Each PokeStop gives you items and 50xp, plus there are usually many more Pokemon in these areas. Activate a Lucky Egg and an Incense if you know you'll have at least 30 minutes to maximize your experience and Pokemon encounters! Keep walking to ensure you'll hatch some Eggs, too.
