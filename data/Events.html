Although no details about events have been revealed, the original Pokemon GO trailer included a scene featuring a "Raid." In this scene, a timer counts down in the background of Times Square in New York, and declares "Defeat Mewtwo."

A girl playing Pokemon GO joins a crowd rushing toward a specific spot seemingly to participate in the event, which has a 10 minute timer. 

Because of these scene, it's theorized that events may involve real-time, real-location "Raids" that require the cooperation of everyone.
