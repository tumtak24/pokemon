Pokemon GO features achievement Medals that track your overall progress in the game, and will reward you with bronze, silver, or gold medals depending on how far along with the medal you've progressed. Unlocking a medal will also grant a small amount of XP when earned.

Below is a list of medals, and the known requirements to get different tiers of those medals. Note that certain medals do not show up until you start fulfilling the requirements - like finding a certain type of Pokemon.
