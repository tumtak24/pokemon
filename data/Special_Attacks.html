Special Attack are the second attack each Pokemon has in Pokemon Go. There are up to three different kinds of attacks each Pokemon can have. 

To use a special attack, you'll have to charge a bar that varies in size and number by using the Pokemon's normal attack. Once a bar is charged, press down on the enemy for a few seconds and your Pokemon will charge its special attack.

Using a special attack can give your Pokemon an edge in battle, but it also leaves them vulnerable to a barrage of attacks from the enemy.
