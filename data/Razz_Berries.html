The Razz Berries are obtainable Items in Pokemon Go.

Razz Berries are items that start appearing at later levels when more difficult pokemon to catch start showing up - and are used to catch these rare pokemon.

When encountering a wild pokemon, if the pokemon has a high Combat Power(CP) level, or is rare, the circle for throwing will often be a darker color, from orange to red, indicating the pokemon may escape when caught - or run away. The player can then use a Razz Berry first to toss at the Pokemon to get it more inclined to stay in the next Poke Ball thrown at them.

Razz Berries cannot be purchased, but will start appearing in PokeStops and as Level Rewards after level 8.
