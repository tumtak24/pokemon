The Potions are obtainable Items in Pokemon Go. They heal a pokemon for 20 health points.

Potions are used to heal Pokemon injured in Gym Battles whether training in a friendly gym, or being worn down from fighting a rival gym. Potions can only be used if the Pokemon is still up and has not fainted - which only rival gyms are capable of. Friendly gyms can never cause your pokemon to faint.

Unlike other items, Potions are only found by visiting PokeStops after the trainer has reached level 5 - at which point they are able to access and fight in gyms.

Potions can also be gained upon reaching certain Levels and getting rewards - like level 5 and beyond.
