The portable device called the Pokemon Go Plus is designed to work together with Pokemon Go. The Pokemon Go Plus connects to your smartphone via Bluetooth low energy, and will give you notifications about in-game events using an LED and vibration.

Additionally, the button on the Pokemon Go Plus can be used to perform simple tasks in-game such as catching Pokemon or collecting items at a PokeStop.

According to the device's Amazon listing the Pokemon Go Plus allows you to: "Collect Poke Balls, Berries, Pokemon Eggs and other items at set PokeStop locations without having to look at your smart phone."

The device also lets you catch Pokemon by simply pressing a button to throw a Poke Ball, but will only allow you to use this simplified method if you've caught the Pokemon before. If it hasn't been added to your Pokedex yet, you'll still have to use your smart phone after the Pokemon GO Plus notifies you that the Pokemon is in range.

Availability of Pokemon Go Plus is currently limited in the United States.
