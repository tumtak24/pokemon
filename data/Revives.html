The Revives are obtainable Items in Pokemon Go.

Revives are used when the player's pokemon have been defeated challenging the rival team's Gym, at which point the pokemon will run out of health and be unusable. Using a revive in the item menu will restore half of the pokemon's health and allow them to fight once more. Note that when training against a friendly gym to raise prestige, pokemon cannot be knocked out.

Revives can be earned by visiting PokeStops after level 5 is reached - the level trainers can start battling at gyms. They are also rewarded by gaining Levels after level 5 and beyond.
