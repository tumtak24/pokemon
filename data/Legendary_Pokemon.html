In Pokemon Go, the promise of finding Legendary Pokemon is a tantalizing subject, but also a bit of mystery currently.

As of writing, there are no known obtainable Legendary Pokemon in Pokemon Go, but evidence of their existence can be found in certain places.

Rumored Legendary Pokemon

  • Articuno, Zapdos, and Moltres are three legendary bird pokemon that appeared in the Kanto region, and although they have not been found in the game yet, each of the three Gym Teams symbolize one of the birds (Team Mystic's mascot is Articuno, Team Insight's mascot is Zapdos, and Team Valor's mascot is Moltres)
  • Mewtwo is a secret legendary pokemon that was an altered clone of Mew. While Mewtwo has not been sighted, he did make an appearance in the cinematic trailer for Pokemon Go, which may hint at his eventual reveal, either only to high level trainer or during events.
  • Mew is a psychic legendary pokemon that so far has neither been mentioned nor hinted at in Pokemon Go. Whether he will make an appearance remains to be seen.
