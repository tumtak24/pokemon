The Shop is the in app store for purchasing items in Pokemon Go. As a free to play game, there are many items you can obtain via other means for free, but may take more time.

For those with disposable income, the shop allows you to bypass such roadblocks so you can get out there and keep catching Pokemon or battling trainers.


Let's take a look!

PokeCoins

All items in Pokemon Go are purchased with the currency called PokeCoins (except the PokeCoins themselves). The PokeCoins can be sometimes gained in game, but otherwise they are purchased using real money, and the prices are listed below.

Note that you can get PokeCoins in game by assigning a Pokemon to defend a Gym, and clicking on the shield icon in the Shop window. Coin amount will vary on the amount of Pokemon assigned, and can only be redeemed once every 21 hours.

Lure Modules

Lure Modules attract Pokemon to PokeStops for 30 minutes. Other players can benefit from your use of them.

You can even see when a PokeStop is under the influence of a Lure Module on the map by looking for the showering of pink petals, and interacting with the PokeStop will have an icon that details who used the lure. Prices are listed below.
