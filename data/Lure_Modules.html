This page contains information on Lure Modules, a type of Chip in Pokemon Go.

Lures are purchasable Items that work like more powerful Incense, in that they are attached to a specific location - a PokeStop of your choosing. They will attract a greater number and variety of pokemon to the attached Pokestop.

How to Use a Lure Module

When deployed, any PokeStop under the influence can be seen on the map with a shower of pink petals around the stop. This can be seen by all players, as Pokemon attracted to the location can be found by any player at that location - making it a great item to use with friends. A new Pokemon will show up approximately every 3 minutes.

Lure Modules can be purchased from the Shop either one for 100 PokeCoins, or 8 Modules for 680 PokeCoins. Additionally, when the trainer reaches certain levels - like level 8, they will also receive a free Lure Module. See the Level Rewards page to see which levels reward Lure Modules.
