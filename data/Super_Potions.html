The Super Potions are obtainable Items in Pokemon Go. They heal a pokemon for 250 health points.

Super Potions are more advanced versions of Potions used to heal Pokemon injured in Gym Battles whether training in a friendly gym, or being worn down from fighting a rival gym. Potions can only be used if the Pokemon is still up and has not fainted - which only rival gyms are capable of. Friendly gyms can never cause your pokemon to faint.

Unlike other items, Super Potions are only found by visiting PokeStops after the trainer has reached level 10.

Super Potions can also be gained upon reaching certain Levels and getting rewards - like level 10 and beyond.
